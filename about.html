Explore. Innovate. Achieve.

Dedicated to making outdoor adventures accessible to all.

Founded in 1976 in France, Decathlon has been revolutionizing outdoor gear by combining cutting-edge innovation with unbeatable affordability. With a presence in over 70 countries, we aim to inspire a healthier, happier, and more sustainable lifestyle for everyone.

Innovation is in Our DNA

We design, test, and perfect all our products at our Mountain and Cycling design centers and R&D lab facilities in Europe. Outdoor enthusiasts from around the world inform our engineering team, and we design our products with the help of pro athletes and industry leaders.

High-Performance Products without the Premium Price

Decathlon is the world’s largest sporting goods retailer, with over 1,700 stores worldwide. With our vertical business model, including research and development facilities, production, and supply chain in-house, we can offer high-performing products without the premium price.

For People and Planet

Protecting our planet for future generations isn’t just something we aspire to—we’re actively working to reduce our environmental footprint. How? By eliminating excess packaging and striving to decrease Decathlon's total CO2 emissions by at least 20% by 2026.

Our Core Values

At Decathlon, we champion excellence, innovation, and inclusivity. Every product is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, performance, and sustainability.
